Top three activities to do in Melbourne

Visit trendy cafes

Does Melbourne have the best coffee in the world? Melbourne has been voted the city with the world's best coffee by travel website booking.com, beating Rome, Vienna and Sydney to top place. MELBOURNE has beaten Rome, Vienna and Sydney in being judged to have the best coffee in the world.

Take a stroll along the streets

The City of Melbourne is already a great place to walk and has a vision to become one of the world's most walk-friendly cities. Get to know Melbourne from the ground up; enjoy walks in Melbourne's city streets, explore Melbourne's art and sculptures and try walks along Melbourne's Yarra River and walks in Melbourne's beautiful parks and gardens.

Ride a tram

Trams are a major form of public transport in Melbourne, the capital city of the state of Victoria, Australia. As of May 2017, the Melbourne tramway network consists of 250 kilometres (160 miles) of double track, 493 trams, 24 routes, and 1,763 tram stops. The system is the largest operational urban tram network in the world.
